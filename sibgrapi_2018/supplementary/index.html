Discussion


The following scenes, as discussed in section IV-A of our work, present a few renderer-related issues.

Scene 7, Little Lamp, presents different coloring in the Mitsuba image due to usage of the sun directive. The LuxRender image also presented issues due to a known problem with the distant light directive.

Scene 8, The Breakfast Room, also presents different coloring in the Mitsuba image.

Scene 9, Veach MIS, is an example of the problem faced when converting metal coloring into LuxRender.
